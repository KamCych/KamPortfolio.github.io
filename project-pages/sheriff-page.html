    • Sheriff

    How to play:

    • Up arrow - Jump
    • Down arrow - Duck
    • Space - Lasso, start game
    • Esc - Quit

    Sheriff is an endless arcade game in which you capture bandits in the Wild West! Dodge obstacles and throw your lasso at the outlaws to catch them and rack up score! Also part of a collaborative 3D game university project.






    Development

    The thing I had in mind during the whole development process, was an authentic arcade game experience.
